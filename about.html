Our Story

Hi, I'm Brandie.

I am a full-time healthcare leader, a mom and grandma, and the creative mind behind these designs. Life is busy, loud, and sometimes overwhelming—but creating has always been my way to slow things down and bring something beautiful into the chaos.

The Sheets & Co Studio was started as a way to help support my family during a season where we became a one-income household, and it’s grown into something I truly love. Every piece you see here is made with intention—because I know the things we buy aren’t just “things”... they hold meaning, memories, and emotion.

I specialize in custom engraved and handcrafted designs that are personal, thoughtful, and made to last.

Thank you for supporting my small business—it truly means more than you know.
